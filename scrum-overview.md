2. Scrum Events
For the simulated 1-sprint (2 weeks, e.g., starting early January 2025), events are planned as:

Sprint Planning: Day 1, 10:00 AM (2 hours). Select backlog items, set sprint goal, and build sprint backlog. All team members.
Daily Scrum: Daily at 9:00 AM (15 minutes, virtual). Share progress, plans, and issues. Developers and Scrum Master.
Sprint Review: End of sprint, 2:00 PM (1 hour). Demo increment, gather feedback. Full team and stakeholders.
Sprint Retrospective: Post-review, 3:30 PM (45 minutes). Reflect on sprint and identify improvements. Internal team.

3. Scrum Artifacts

Product Backlog: Prioritized list of user stories and tasks for the app, refined by Product Owner.
Sprint Backlog: Actionable tasks from the product backlog for this sprint, updated by the team.
Increment: Deliverable prototype at sprint end, meeting the Definition of Done (e.g., basic task management features).
